Welcome to my corner of the internet. The idea of a static blog was inspired by my good friend Edward [(edho.me)](http://edho.me) and serves as a space for my thoughts and reflections. 

Despite the best of intentions, posts here will inevitably be sporadic. Topics will include medicine, music, why things are the way they are, and what the point of this all might be. 


### The history of henryhe.me

This blog is built on [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/), an open-source static site generator written in the programming language Go. 

The first theme I originally chose in 2021 was [Hugo Vitae](https://github.com/dataCobra/hugo-vitae), and then in 2026 switched to [Stack](https://stack.cai.im/), because of its simplicity and organizational features. The code for this website can be found [here](https://github.com/henryhe1/space-vitae).

If you're curious, this was what my [old website](https://henryhe.me/old-website) looked like, built mostly with [Bootstrap](https://getbootstrap.com/). I created it in Nov 2015 after I got this domain name free for 1 year by attending my university hackathon.
